About

Hi! I am a Kenyan girl passionate about building solutions to societal problems using Computer Science. I am currently a rising sophomore Computer Science student at Northwestern University. I am proficient in C++, python, Racket, C, HTML, CSS, and Matlab. I have also started learning Android Development over the summer on my own. I am interested in software development internships. Activities

Fanaka Founder and Project Manager

Over the summer, I run a project: Fanaka, in rural Kenya that exposed about 4400 Kenyan high school students to educational opportunities in Africa and USA. Fanaka was inspired by the inadequacy of resources for students from rural regions. Fanaka was generously funded by Davis Projects for Peace through Northwestern Buffett Institute.
